## Why sponsor

legba is maintained by a single author. Sponsorship directly funds:

- **New protocol plugins** and bugfixes for the ones you already use.
- **Performance work** to keep legba measurably faster than the alternatives ([benchmark](benchmark.md)).
- **AI agent integration** through the [Model Context Protocol server](mcp.md), so legba stays usable from Claude Desktop, Claude Code, and custom agents.
- **Documentation, recipes, and worked examples** like the ones on this site.
- **Test infrastructure** (Docker compose stacks, mock servers, CI runners) so credential testing tools can be developed and validated reproducibly.

If your company runs pentest engagements with legba, sponsoring is the most direct way to keep the tool you depend on healthy and current.

Open an issue or DM through any of the above channels and we can work out the details.

## Other ways to support

If sponsorship is not an option, these are all genuinely useful:

- **Star and watch** [the repository](https://github.com/evilsocket/legba) to help visibility.
- **Report issues** and reproduction steps when something breaks against a server in the wild.
- **Send PRs**, especially for new protocols, plugins, and bugfixes.
- **Write recipes** for attack flows you find yourself running often, and contribute them back.
- **Share benchmarks** if you have reproducible numbers against Medusa, Ncrack, Patator, or other tools so the [comparison page](comparison.md) can stay accurate.
